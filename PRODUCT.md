# Katy Ilicheva Portfolio

## Product Purpose
Personal portfolio for Katy Ilicheva, a Munich-based Film and SFX Makeup Artist working across film, fashion, character design, prosthetics, and commercial productions.

## Users
Production teams, photographers, stylists, fashion clients, commercial producers, and creative directors who need a quick visual read of Katy's range and a direct way to contact her.

## Register
brand

## Tone
Precise, cinematic, confident, visual-first. The site should feel like a professional makeup artist's book, not a template landing page.

## Strategic Principles
- Keep the Tilda site's simple structure and direct navigation.
- Let the work carry the page through strong image rhythm and restrained copy.
- Make contact links obvious and correct.
- Keep the site portable for GitHub Pages and future custom domain setup.

## Anti-References
- Generic SaaS landing page sections.
- Heavy cards, feature grids, testimonials, fake metrics, or service pricing blocks.
- Overly decorative gradients, beige palettes, and glass effects.
